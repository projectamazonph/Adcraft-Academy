# AdCraft Course Syllabus

> Operational course syllabus for the AdCraft Operator Certification. This is not a passive reading list; it is a progression of simulations, decision gates, and skill validations following the "Do The Thing" pedagogical model.

> **Source**: Qwen chat — "AdCraft Product Development Gaps" (2026-06-03)
> **MVP Scope**: Modules 0, 1, 4, 6, 7 are P0 priority. All other modules are post-MVP.

---

## Certification Overview

- **Total Duration**: Self-Paced (Est. 40-60 Hours)
- **Prerequisites**: None (Module 0 calibrates to experience level)
- **Certification**: AdCraft Certified PPC Operator (Level 1-5)
- **Pass Threshold**: 80% on Capstone for Level 4 (Strategist); 92% + Roleplay Mastery for Level 5 (Commander)

---

## Phase 1: Foundations & Readiness (Weeks 1-2)

**Goal**: Prevent wasted spend before a single campaign launches.

### Module 0: PPC Game Onboarding — "Choose Your ASIN" (🔴 P0)

**Core Interaction**: Select simulated ASIN, set KPI targets, configure difficulty mode. AI Mentor goal alignment chat.

**Screens**:
- Role Selection (4 cards: Junior Specialist, Brand Owner, Agency AM, PPC Strategist)
- Difficulty Mode (Beginner / Agency / Savage)
- Product Selector (5 product cards + Custom ASIN)
- Goal Alignment (AI Mentor chat with quick-reply chips)
- KPI Setup Worksheet (Interactive calculator with live outputs)

**Unlockable Tool**: PPC KPI Setup Worksheet

### Module 1: Amazon PPC Foundations — "The Map Before the Maze" (🔴 P0)

**Core Interaction**: Metric Mixer Game: Drag-and-drop formulas to build ACoS, ROAS, TACoS. "Budget Bonfire" waste identification.

**Lessons**:
- 1.1: What Is Amazon PPC? (SP, SB, SD overview)
- 1.2: ACoS vs ROAS vs TACoS (Gold-standard lesson, see content-production-kit.md)
- 1.3: CPC, CTR, CVR — The Action Metrics

**Unlockable Tool**: Metrics Cheat Sheet & Calculator

### Module 2: Listing Readiness Clinic — "Don't Advertise a Leaky Bucket" (🟡 P1)

**Core Interaction**: PDP Audit Simulator: Diagnose a leaky listing (images, reviews, price). Decide: Launch, Delay, Brand Defense Only, Fix First, or Throttle. Listing Leak Meter: Visualizes how bad PDP quality drains ad spend.

**Assessment**: Correct diagnosis of listing risks + appropriate launch decision.

**Unlockable Tools**: Listing Readiness Checklist, Pre-Scale Approval Form, PDP Audit Template

### Module 3: Keyword Research Lab — "Mine, Filter, Classify, Prioritize" (🟡 P1)

**Core Interaction**: Keyword Sorting Table: Tag 50 keywords by intent, campaign type, match type, priority. Keyword Goblin Detector: Catch irrelevant terms hidden in a list before they get budget.

**Assessment**: 40/50 correct classifications.

**Unlockable Tools**: Keyword Master List Template, Intent Classification Worksheet, Cerebro Import Guide

**Phase 1 Gate**: Must score >80% on Listing Readiness Audit and correctly classify 40/50 keywords to proceed.

---

## Phase 2: Architecture & Launch (Weeks 3-4)

**Goal**: Build structurally sound campaigns that scale without cannibalization.

### Module 4: Campaign Architecture Builder — "Build the Machine" (🔴 P0)

**Core Interaction**: Visual Builder: Drag campaign blocks into lifecycle portfolios. Naming Validator: Real-time feedback on missing fields, length, wrong family. Cannibalization Alarm: Blares when same term appears in multiple places without negatives.

**Lessons**:
- Campaign Taxonomy (16 types defined in PRD)
- Separation Logic (Branded/Generic, Research/Performance, Exact/Broad)
- Naming Convention: [BRAND]_[COUNTRY]_[STAGE]_[TYPE]_[MATCH]_[THEME]_[YYYYMM]

**Assessment**: Valid 10-campaign structure with zero naming errors.

**Unlockable Tools**: Naming Generator, Architecture Map, Portfolio Budget Allocator, QA Checklist

### Module 5: Launch Strategy Simulator — "Day 1 to Month 2" (🟡 P1)

**Core Interaction**: Timeline Board: Place campaign cards into weeks. Consequence Engine: "You built a retargeting net before any fish swam by." / "Hero keyword selected with no proof. That is vibes with a budget."

**Assessment**: Correct sequencing of all 5 phases.

**Unlockable Tools**: 35-Day Launch Calendar, Launch QA Checklist, Rollout Kanban Board

### Module 6: Bidding Lab — "The CPC Ceiling Is Lava" (🔴 P0)

**Core Interaction**: Interactive Calculator: Inputs (Price, Target ACoS, CVR) to Output (CPC_max). Bid Elevator Mini-Game: Move bids up/down. Simulates impressions, clicks, spend, sales, ACoS, rank pressure. Scenario Cards: Increase/Decrease/Hold/Investigate based on data.

**Lessons**: CPC_max Formula, Lifecycle Bid Settings, Rank Push Exceptions

**Assessment**: 5/5 bid adjustment scenarios passed.

**Unlockable Tools**: CPC_max Calculator, Bid Decision Tree, Placement Multiplier Sheet, Bid Change Log

**Phase 2 Gate**: Must build a valid 10-campaign structure with zero naming errors and pass 5/5 bid adjustment scenarios.

---

## Phase 3: Optimization & Diagnostics (Weeks 5-6)

**Goal**: Turn data into profitable decisions under pressure.

### Module 7: Search Term Triage Arena — "Promote, Negate, Hold, Investigate" (🔴 P0)

**Core Interaction**: STR Data Grid with 40+ rows of synthetic data. Actions: Promote, Negate Exact/Phrase, Watchlist, Reduce, Pause, Investigate. Confidence Meter: Enforces data sufficiency before allowing action. Feedback Panel: Explains why based on triggered rules.

**Lessons**: Promotion Thresholds (20+ clicks + conversion OR CVR >= 0.8x median), Negation Logic, Exact vs Phrase Negatives, Cannibalization Prevention.

**Assessment**: >85% triage accuracy.

**Unlockable Tools**: Triage Worksheet, Weekly Harvesting SOP, Negative Keyword Blueprint, Promotion Import Sheet

### Module 8: Budget, Pacing & Rank Push — "Growth Without Chaos" (🟡 P1)

**Core Interaction**: Inventory Throttle Scenario: 12 days cover + maxing budget. Tempting move: increase. Correct move: throttle critical campaigns only. Rank Push Timer: Countdown clock with TACoS risk meter.

**Assessment**: Correct budget allocation + safe rank push execution.

**Unlockable Tools**: Budget Pacing Dashboard, Rank Push Approval Form, TACoS Guardrail Tracker, Inventory Throttle Checklist

### Module 9: Creative & Ad Type Studio — "Match Ad to Mission" (🟡 P1)

**Core Interaction**: Creative Grader: Upload/select ad concept. Scored on keyword-message match, benefit clarity, policy risk, funnel fit. Policy Checker: Flags misleading claims, TM misuse, PDP misalignment.

**Assessment**: Correct ad type selection for 5 missions + passing creative grade.

**Unlockable Tools**: SB Creative Checklist, Video Storyboard Template, Creative Test Tracker, Objective-to-Format Matrix

### Module 10: Troubleshooting War Room — "Diagnose Before You Touch Bids" (🟡 P1)

**Core Interaction**: Case File Diagnosis: Enter case ("ACoS jumped 24% to 58%, CVR dropped 35%, reviews 4.3 to 3.9"). Correct diagnosis: Listing/Review issue, not bid issue. "Don't Blame the Bid Button" Mechanic: Penalizes immediate bid reduction when root cause is PDP/pricing/inventory.

**Assessment**: 3/3 cases correctly diagnosed.

**Unlockable Tools**: Triage Decision Tree, Weekly Review Checklist, RAG Escalation Sheet, Root Cause Template

**Phase 3 Gate**: Must achieve >85% triage accuracy and correctly diagnose 3/3 troubleshooting cases.

---

## Phase 4: Communication & Mastery (Weeks 7-8)

**Goal**: Prove operational readiness and stakeholder management.

### Module 11: Reporting & Client Communication — "Make Data Make Sense" (🟡 P1)

**Core Interaction**: AI Client Roleplay: Skeptical AI asks "Why did ACoS go up?" Learner responds. Scored on Accuracy, Calmness, Clarity, Next-Steps, Overpromising. Client Heat Gauge: Rises with vague/defensive answers.

**Assessment**: Passing roleplay score (>80%).

**Unlockable Tools**: Weekly Report Template, Monthly Deck Outline, Optimization Changelog, Email Swipes, Quarterly Review Template

### Module 12: Capstone — "Run the Account" (🟡 P1)

**Simulation Parameters**: 3 ASINs, 90 days history, STRs, keyword file, structure issues, budget constraints, inventory risk, competitor pressure, client objective.

**Tasks**: Audit, Build, Name, Allocate, Calculate CPC_max, Sequence, Analyze 14-day, Promote/Negate, Adjust Bids, Write Summary, Oral Defense.

**Grading Rubric**:
- Listing: 10%
- Keywords: 15%
- Structure: 15%
- Naming: 10%
- Bids: 15%
- Triage: 15%
- Budget/Rank: 10%
- Comms: 10%

**Pass**: 80%. **Advanced**: 92% + Roleplay Mastery.

## Gamification Mechanics

| Mechanic | Trigger Module(s) | Learning Reinforcement |
|----------|-------------------|----------------------|
| ACoS Monster | All Sims | Grows with wasteful decisions. Visualizes cumulative cost. |
| Keyword Forge | Mod 3, 7 | Turn raw terms into polished exact-match weapons. |
| Budget Shield | Mod 8 | Protects profitable campaigns from bad reallocations. |
| Cannibalization Alarm | Mod 4, 7 | Blares when same term in multiple places w/o negatives. |
| Rank Push Timer | Mod 6, 8 | Countdown + TACoS risk meter. Enforces timeboxing. |
| Listing Leak Meter | Mod 2 | Shows PDP quality draining ad spend. |
| Client Heat Gauge | Mod 11 | Rises with vague reporting. Trains emotional regulation. |
| Boss Battles | End of Each World | "ACoS Spike," "No-Delivery Mystery," "Branded Black Hole." |
| Replay Mode | Post-Sim | Safe exploration of alternate outcomes. |
