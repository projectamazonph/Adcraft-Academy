# Welcome to AdCraft: Amazon PPC Command Center

You're about to embark on a journey that will transform you from an Amazon advertising novice into a confident, data-driven PPC strategist. AdCraft is not a passive course — it's a **hands-on training simulator** where you'll make real decisions in realistic scenarios and learn from the outcomes.

## What You'll Learn

By completing the AdCraft program, you will:

- **Understand every PPC metric** — Know what ACoS, TACoS, ROAS, CPC, CTR, and CVR mean, how they're calculated, and what they tell you about campaign health
- **Build complete campaign structures** — Create Sponsored Products, Brands, and Display campaigns with proper keyword targeting, bid strategies, and budgets
- **Master bidding strategy** — Know when to bid aggressively, when to pull back, and how to calculate your maximum profitable bid for any keyword
- **Triaging search terms like a pro** — Quickly identify which search terms to keep, negate, or optimize based on performance data

## How AdCraft Works

AdCraft uses a **"Do The Thing"** learning model. Instead of watching videos and taking quizzes, you'll:

1. **Learn the concepts** through interactive lessons (like this one)
2. **Apply them immediately** in realistic simulations
3. **Get AI-powered feedback** on your decisions
4. **Track your progress** with XP, levels, and completion badges

### The Three Core Simulations

| Simulation | What You'll Do | Module |
|-----------|----------------|--------|
| **Campaign Builder** | Design complete campaign structures from scratch | Campaign Architecture |
| **Bid Elevator** | Make bidding decisions across real-world scenarios | Bidding Lab |
| **STR Triage Arena** | Analyze and act on 20+ search terms under time pressure | Search Term Triage |

## Your First Mission

After this onboarding module, you'll dive into **Module 1: Foundations**, where you'll learn the essential PPC metrics that form the backbone of every advertising decision. Every module builds on the last, and by the end, you'll have the skills to manage real Amazon PPC campaigns with confidence.

> **Important**: AdCraft is a simulation environment. No real money is spent, no real campaigns are affected. This is your sandbox to experiment, make mistakes, and learn without risk.
