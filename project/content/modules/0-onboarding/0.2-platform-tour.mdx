# Platform Tour & Navigation

Now that you know what AdCraft is, let's explore the platform so you can navigate it confidently. This lesson covers every section of the AdCraft interface and explains how each piece fits into your learning journey.

## The Sidebar — Your Command Center

The sidebar on the left is your primary navigation tool. It contains four sections, each representing a different aspect of your PPC training:

| Section | Purpose | What You'll Find |
|---------|---------|-----------------|
| **Dashboard** | Your home base | Welcome card, stats, module progress, simulation cards, quick actions |
| **Modules** | Structured learning | 5 learning modules with lesson breakdowns and progress tracking |
| **Simulations** | Hands-on practice | 3 interactive simulations (Campaign Builder, Bid Elevator, STR Triage) |
| **AI Mentor** | On-demand coaching | Chat-based AI assistant that answers PPC questions with grounded advice |

The sidebar also shows your current **XP** and **level** at the bottom. As you complete lessons and simulations, these numbers will increase, reflecting your growing expertise.

## Dashboard — The Big Picture

The Dashboard is the first thing you see when you open AdCraft. It provides a high-level overview of your progress and quick access to everything:

- **Welcome card** — Shows your current level and XP progress toward the next level
- **Stats row** — Key metrics about your training progress (simulations completed, average score, lessons done)
- **Module cards** — Visual overview of all 5 modules with progress bars and status badges
- **Simulation cards** — Quick-launch buttons for each simulation
- **Quick actions** — One-click shortcuts to continue learning, start a simulation, or ask the AI Mentor

The Dashboard is designed to answer the question: *"What should I do next?"* At a glance, you can see where you left off and what's available to you.

## Modules — Structured Learning Path

The Modules view shows all five learning modules in sequence. Each module contains multiple lessons, and you must complete them in order. Here's the full curriculum:

| Module | Title | Lessons | Focus |
|--------|-------|---------|-------|
| 0 | Onboarding | 3 | Platform orientation, first simulation intro |
| 1 | Foundations | 5 | PPC metrics, CPC, ACoS, TACoS, ROAS |
| 4 | Campaign Architecture | 4 | Sponsored Products, Brands, Display |
| 6 | Bidding Lab | 4 | Bid strategies, position economics, budget pacing |
| 7 | Search Term Triage | 3 | Negative keywords, STR analysis, optimization |

Each module card shows:
- **Progress bar** — How far you've progressed through the module
- **Lesson list** — Every lesson with its duration and completion status
- **Formula Calculator** — Module 1 includes an interactive calculator widget for exploring PPC formulas

> **Important**: Modules must be completed in sequence. Module 1 unlocks after Module 0, Module 4 after Module 1, and so on. This ensures you have the foundational knowledge before tackling advanced topics.

## Simulations — Where Theory Meets Practice

The Simulations view is where you apply what you've learned. Each simulation is unlocked by completing its prerequisite module:

| Simulation | Prerequisite | What You'll Do |
|-----------|-------------|----------------|
| **Campaign Builder** | Module 4 | Build a complete campaign structure with keywords, bids, and budgets |
| **Bid Elevator** | Module 6 | Make 10 bidding decisions with instant feedback on accuracy |
| **STR Triage Arena** | Module 7 | Triage 20 search terms with keep/pause/negate decisions |

Each simulation has four phases:
1. **Briefing** — Mission context, product details, and key formulas to remember
2. **Action** — The interactive simulation where you make decisions
3. **Scoring** — Animated score reveal with criteria breakdown
4. **Review** — Detailed feedback on each decision with educational explanations

Simulations can be replayed as many times as you like. Your best score is recorded for XP purposes, but reviewing your mistakes is where the real learning happens.

## AI Mentor — Your PPC Coach

The AI Mentor is a chat-based assistant that answers your PPC questions. It's available at any time from the sidebar, and it has context about:
- Which module and lesson you're currently working on
- Your simulation scores and progress
- PPC rules and best practices from the AdCraft curriculum

The Mentor is especially useful when:
- You don't understand why a simulation scored you a certain way
- You want clarification on a metric or formula
- You need help deciding between two strategies in a simulation
- You want to understand the "why" behind a PPC best practice

> **Tip**: The AI Mentor references specific PPC rules and curriculum content in its responses. If it cites a rule, you can trust it — it's grounded in the AdCraft knowledge base, not making things up.

## XP and Leveling System

AdCraft uses an XP (Experience Points) system to track your progress:

| Action | XP Earned |
|--------|-----------|
| Complete a lesson | 50–75 XP |
| Complete a simulation | Score x 2 XP (up to 200 XP) |
| Perfect score bonus | +50 XP |
| Daily streak | +25 XP |
| First attempt bonus | +30 XP |

You level up every 500 XP. Each level unlocks new content and reflects your growing expertise:

| Level | Title | XP Required |
|-------|-------|-------------|
| 1 | Trainee | 0 |
| 2 | Analyst | 500 |
| 3 | Strategist | 1,000 |
| 4 | Commander | 1,500 |
| 5 | Expert | 2,000 |

## Keyboard Shortcuts

For power users, AdCraft supports these keyboard shortcuts:

| Shortcut | Action |
|----------|--------|
| `Ctrl + 1` | Go to Dashboard |
| `Ctrl + 2` | Go to Modules |
| `Ctrl + 3` | Go to Simulations |
| `Ctrl + 4` | Go to AI Mentor |
